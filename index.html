Final Year Project

Neuro Assist Mobility System

In my final year project, I developed a Neuro-Assist Mobility System that uses AI to assist individuals with mobility challenges. By training a CNN model on a custom dataset collected from my university, I combined deep learning with Mediapipe for real-time hand gesture detection. Our system recognizes five essential gestures: forward, backward, left, right, and stop, enabling precise wheelchair control. While this demonstration uses a prototype instead of a full-scale wheelchair, it showcases the potential of AI in enhancing assistive technologies.
